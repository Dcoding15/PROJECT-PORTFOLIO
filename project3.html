Home - The welcoming gateway. It instantly showcases all available doctors, empowering patients to immediately see who is ready to provide care and begin their journey to wellness.

Login - The secure entry point. It acts as a vigilant gatekeeper, verifying identities and seamlessly guiding patients and doctors to their own personalized command centers.

Registration (Both Patient and Doctor) - The effortless onboarding hub. It smoothly captures all essential details, transforming new users into verified members of the healthcare community with a swift and simple process.

Patient Dashboard - The patient's personal health command center. This hub displays their vital information and all doctor communications, and provides powerful tools to send messages and instantly book new appointments, putting them in complete control of their care.

Doctor Dashboard - The doctor's professional mission control. It manages their availability status, displays all patient messages, and equips them to send crucial updates, ensuring they are always connected and informed.

Booking Appointment - The seamless scheduling engine. This page empowers patients to instantly secure their appointment with their chosen doctor, finalizing the time and providing essential medical history in one decisive action.
