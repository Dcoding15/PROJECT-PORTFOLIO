Home – Displays all available doctors so patients can quickly find medical professionals and begin booking appointments.

Login – Authenticates both patients and doctors and directs them to their personalized dashboards.

Registration – Allows both patients and doctors to create accounts by submitting essential personal and professional details.

Patient Dashboard – Shows patient information, doctor messages, and provides tools to book new appointments.

Doctor Dashboard – Allows doctors to manage availability, view patient requests, and respond to patient communications.

Booking Appointment – Enables patients to select a doctor, schedule a visit, and submit relevant medical information.
